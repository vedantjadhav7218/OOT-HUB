Information about OTT Platform

OTT means Over The Top platform. It is an online streaming service that provides movies, web series, and TV shows using internet without cable connection. Users can watch anytime anywhere using mobile, laptop, or smart TV.

History of OTT Platform

OTT platforms started growing after fast internet services. Netflix started online streaming in 2007 which changed the entertainment industry. After that many companies started their own OTT platforms. During COVID-19 pandemic OTT usage increased very fast because people watched movies online.

Examples of OTT Platforms

Netflix – Popular OTT platform for movies, web series and shows worldwide.

Amazon Prime Video – Streaming service with movies, series and original content.

Disney+ Hotstar – Famous in India for movies, cricket, and TV shows.

Jio Hotstar – Indian OTT platform for movies, live cricket, TV shows and web series.

Sony LIV – Indian OTT platform for TV shows, movies, sports and web series.
